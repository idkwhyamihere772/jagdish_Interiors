Artisanal Method

Our Seamless Work Method

Unlike cookie-cutter home decor shops, every contract we undertake follows a structured architectural lifecycle from site laser survey to final handover.

01

Site Assessment

Our surveyor visits your residence, site, or commercial building to take precise structural laser measurements and assess window layout structures.

02

Material Presentation

We present curated physical catalogs of PVC textures, curtains fabrics, flooring shades, and glass film patterns at your site for exact alignment with your lighting.

03

Bespoke Fabrication

Frames are built, curtains stitched, and PVC louvers trimmed exactly to custom millimeter clearances inside our specialized production studio.

04

Turnkey Handover

Our skilled logistics crew executes complete floor laying, curtain rod hanging, PVC mounting, or glass film sticking with zero visual flaws.
